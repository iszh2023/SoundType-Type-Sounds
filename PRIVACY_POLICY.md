SoundType Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-14

What SoundType does
- SoundType plays a short sound when you press keys, based on the “series” you select in the floating SoundType window.

Data we collect
- We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.
- We do not send your browsing history, keystrokes, or page content to any server.

Data stored on your device
SoundType stores only settings needed to run the extension. These are saved using Chrome’s extension storage:
- Selected sound series
- Volume level
- Theme (light/dark)
- Window open/closed state
- Window position and size

Audio files
- If you add your own audio files to the extension folder (for example, under `sounds/<series>/`), those files stay on your device and are only loaded locally by the extension.

Third-party services
- None.

Security
- The extension does not execute remote code and does not load scripts from external websites.

Contact
- If you need to contact the developer, add an email address here before publishing to the Chrome Web Store.
